Nathan's Hobbies

This is going to be a brief essay on some of the things that I enjoy doing. When I reflect on my hobbies and things I like to do, three things come to mind; music, film, and history. Those are my three favorite fields and recreational pastimes, I will explain what makes them my favorite and why I enjoy them so much.

First, I will begin with music. Music has been my favorite hobby for a long time. I enjoy making music, listening to music, and discussing music. Some of my favorite musical artists are Neil Young, Kraftwerk, and Tom Waits. I like many other artists and bands but those are a few of my favorites. When it comes to making music, I can play the guitar, bass, piano, and the drums. I use most of these instruments while making music.

Next, I will discuss my love of film. My love for movies began a year or two ago, making it one of my newer interests. Some of my favorite movies are 2001 a Space Odyssey, Aguirre: The Wrath of God, and Taxi Driver. Watching movies has quickly become one of my favorite pastimes.

Lastly, is my love for history. My love for history began in elementary school when I read a book on the Aztecs. My favorite area to study is ancient civilizations and theology. I primarily hae studied a lot of European, Middle Eastern, and Mesoamerican history. I plan on learning more about eastern history as well.
